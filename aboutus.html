About Momo Sushi

Welcome to Momo Sushi, where tradition meets creativity. Established in 2018, our restaurant was founded on a passion for fresh ingredients, beautiful presentation, and bold flavors. From classic rolls to signature sushi burritos, we’re proud to serve a unique fusion of modern and traditional Japanese and Korean cuisine.

Our mission is to bring joy and flavor to every dish, whether you're dining in, ordering out, or chatting with our team. Thank you for supporting a local business that truly cares about its community.
