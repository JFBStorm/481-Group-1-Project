Our Story

Momo Sushi began in 2018 as a small neighborhood spot with a simple goal: serve fresh, high-quality sushi in a space where people feel at home. What started with a handful of recipes and a lot of heart has grown into a go-to destination for Ann Arbor sushi lovers looking for something authentic, approachable, and a little different.

Our menu is inspired by a mix of traditional Japanese and Korean dishes, combined with creative modern touches. From classic nigiri and sashimi to sushi burritos and hearty rice and noodle bowls, everything is made with care.

At Momo Sushi, we believe that good food is about more than just ingredients. It's about people — the guests who support us, the team who prepare every plate, and the community we’re proud to be a part of. Whether you’re here for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or a celebration with friends, we’re here to make it special.

Thank you for being part of our journey. We’re excited to keep sharing new flavors and memories with you.

Meet the Team

Sam — Owner & Executive Chef

Founder & Culinary Lead

Originally from Seoul, South Korea, Sam has spent over two decades honing his culinary skills. As the founder of Momo Sushi, he brings his experience and passion to every corner of the kitchen — from designing the menu, to overseeing quality and training.

Andres — Head Chef

Kitchen Operations

Andres brings energy to the kitchen every single day. Known for his big smile, loud laugh, and contagious positivity, he keeps the team motivated and the mood light — even during the dinner rush. His strong culinary skills and upbeat attitude make him a favorite among both staff and regulars. Behind the humor is a chef who takes pride in getting every detail right.

Taylor — Front of House Manager

Hospitality & Experience

Taylor is the first smile you see and the heartbeat of our dining room. She ensures your Momo experience is welcoming, seamless, from the moment you walk in to your last bite.
